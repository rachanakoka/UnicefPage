UNICEF's Youth Foresight Playbook at the Dubai Future Forum




The UNICEF Innocenti Youth Engagement and Strategy team, in collaboration with the Dubai Future Forum, is excited to introduce a two-hour workshop at the Museum of the Future in 2023. Children and young people have been a driving force for change throughout history. It makes sense therefore that they should also be deeply involved in imagining the future â€“ their future, our future, the future of the planet, and that their absence from foresight and decision making spaces greatly hinders our capacity to anticipate how the world is changing. Regardless, children and young people have a right to be part of any conversation about the future they will inherit, and to develop their talents and abilities to the full in order to have agency in a rapidly changing world and bring about the futures they wish for. It is with this sentiment in mind that we are proud to launch UNICEF's brand-new Youth Foresight Playbook. This case study-based toolkit, inspired by UNICEF Innocenti's Youth Foresight Fellowship program launched in 2022 with funding from the Government of Finland through UN Global Pulse, was created for foresight practitioners, youth-focused and youth-led organisations, as well as think tanks and UN agencies, to meaningfully engage youth in foresight, and in decision making spaces using foresight.
